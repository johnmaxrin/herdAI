Hi, I am Herd Ai.

For defense purposes, the project aims to build autonomous swarms of Micro UAVs. The swarms can be tracked over the Internet and can be managed a little bit over the Internet. ASCII, Impact Lab and Department of IT at AJCE are funding this project.

Additionally, we are developing a FANET which helps swarms of such MAVs to share resources, causing battery life to be enhanced and these MAVs to work together. Once the Web application is complete, you will be able to access it here.
